About Me


Hi! I'm Rachel. I'm a student at Bellevue College studying Data Analysis. I previously graduated from Oregon State University with a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology. I'm back in school to gain skills that can help me expand in my career as a Project Manager or Functional Analyst.


When I'm not working or studying, I enjoy baking, antiquing, and staying active! My favorite things to bake are cake and doughnuts. I love having fun with flavors and adapting traditional recipes inspired by my favorite childhood sweets!
I also enjoy thrifting and antiquing. I love interior decoration, and shopping secondhand allows me to do this in an affordable and sustainable way! As a Seattle native, hiking takes up a large part of my pastime. I also enjoy working out by weightlifting.
