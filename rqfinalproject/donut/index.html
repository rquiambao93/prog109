Donut Making 101

Are you interested in learning how to make donuts? This is the page for you!

Getting Started


Many people are surprised to learn that doughnuts are my favorite thing to make. I've created this page so that you can learn to make them, too!


Doughnuts. Who doesn't love them?! During the height of quarantine during the COVID-19 pandemic, while others were making sourdough bread, I was making doughnuts. People often tell me that it sounds difficult to make. Between using yeast and having to fry them, it can sound like a lot of work with little reward. Other Recipes to Try


These are some of my favorite donut recipes. Try them out!

Mochi Donuts

A recipe for glutinous rice flour donuts. A unique, chewy texture while still incredibly light. Try using melted White Rabbit candies for a glaze!

Pastry Cream

A simple yet delicious recipe for vanilla pastry cream. Try updating it with your own unique flavors for donut fillings!

Cake Donuts

These aren't your average cake doughnuts. These are light, airy, and very easy to make! They're made with greek yogurt, so you could actually call them a yonut.
